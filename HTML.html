SYNOPSIS:

Desperate to keep custody of his younger sister, Abby, a troubled young man named Mike Schimidt takes a job as a night security guard at Freddy’s Fazbear’s Pizzeria, a derelict family entertainment center that was abandoned in the 1980s.

PRODUCTION DETAILS:

Directed by Emma Tammi, it's a supernatural horror film. Produced by Blumhouse, it’s an adaptation of the hit video game. Starring Josh Hutcherson, it's a survival thriller. Featuring Jim Henson's Creature Shop, it's a practical effects showcase..
