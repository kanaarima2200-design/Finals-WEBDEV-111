Our Story

Haraya — a Filipino word meaning imagination, dreams, and creative vision. Founded in 2020, Cafe Haraya was born from a dream to create a space where Filipino warmth meets exceptional coffee culture.

Every bean we source, every pastry we bake, and every dish we serve carries the spirit of haraya — thoughtfully crafted to inspire and delight. We believe that a great cup of coffee can spark creativity, meaningful conversations, and lasting memories.
